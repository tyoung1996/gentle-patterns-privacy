Gentle Patterns (“the app”) is designed to support reflection and task follow-through in a calm, non-judgmental way. Your privacy is important, and we aim to collect as little data as possible to provide the app’s core functionality.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Task and reminder content you create in the app
  • Optional interactions with the Coach or reflection features

This information is stored locally on your device and/or securely synced to support app functionality.

Automatically collected information

  • Basic, non-identifying usage data (such as app opens or feature usage) may be collected to improve app stability and experience.
  • Device information required for app functionality (such as operating system version).

Purchases and Payments

If you choose to upgrade to Premium:

  • Payments are processed by Google Play
  • The app does not store or process your payment information
  • Purchase status is managed securely via a third-party service (RevenueCat) to determine access to premium features

How We Use Information

We use collected information only to provide and improve app functionality, enable premium features when applicable, and understand usage patterns to improve the experience.

We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except when required to process subscriptions or app functionality, or when required by law. No data is shared for targeted advertising.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information and minimize data collection. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Gentle Patterns is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You may delete tasks or content within the app at any time.
  • You may uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.
